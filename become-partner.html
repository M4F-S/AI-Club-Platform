External support

We are open to educational partnerships, sponsorships, and student programs from AI companies and organizations that support learning communities. If supported, we plan to use those resources to improve workshops, build student projects, and expand access to AI tools for members.

Any external support will be used in a transparent way and always in service of the club’s educational mission. We welcome partnerships that remain transparent, student-focused, and respectful of 42 Berlin’s rules and data protection expectations.
